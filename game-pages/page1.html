Who am I?

So... The big question on your head. Probably. Is who am I?

You have read my essays, seen my grades and activities and honors, but you're curious. So here you are.

And to be honest, I'm not sure either. If I were asked to tell a story about myself in a few hundred words, I don't think I could do that. Which is why, instead of telling you who I am, I'm going to show you!


Who I'm Not

You see, telling people who you are is hard. But telling people who you're not, is much easier.

After all, it's simpler to think of things that we clearly aren't. For example, I'm not a genius. Not even close. Further from it than Earth from Neptune.

Or a basketball player: I can't think of a time where I managed to score an any pointer. :(
