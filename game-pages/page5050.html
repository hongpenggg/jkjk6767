Coding

Now, as someone who would rather type than talk, I find coding to be a very fulfilling activity. For me, coding is like Minecraft: it's like having the absolute freedom to let your creativity and imagination run wild! You can build crazy projects, design rainbow websites, and create useful little programs!

But coding is not just about fun and games. It's also a powerful tool that can help solve real-world problems. One of the projects I created with an outsized impact on workflows is the LogBot app. Prior to the LogBot web-app, my army battalion stuck to outdated paper logbooks to record vehicle movements and statuses. With LogBot, all these information can be easily accessed and updated online. Not only is it more efficient, it is also more informative. The current LogBot still has a long way to go, as my army friends and I look to build a Telegram bot to further simplify the process. Stay tuned!
